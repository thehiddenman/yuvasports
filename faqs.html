1. General

Yuva Sports is an app for the local amateur players of the world. It allows you to score your local any Sports match and broadcast scores online just like an International match.

It also allows you to add your own team, search players, search teams, arrange a match with local teams, find tournaments around you, analyse your scores, share your achievements and much more…

In one line, Yuva Sports is your new Sports Network!
